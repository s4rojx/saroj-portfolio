---
title: "Garden of Emotions"
date: "2026-01-05"
tags: ["love", "emotions"]
description: "A poem about love and emotions."
---

In the garden where emotions bloom,
A silent song, a sweet perfume.
Words unspoken, a delicate art,
Whispered subtly from the heart.

In glances shared, a hidden dance,
Feelings woven in a shy romance.
Through verses spun in moonlit ink,
Hints of love on petals pink.

A secret garden, our hearts' decree,
Where only you and I hold the key.
In every sigh, a story unfolds,
A love that quietly, tenderly molds.
