---
title: "Echoes of a Hidden Flame"
date: "2026-01-19"
tags: ["love", "unspoken"]
description: "A poem about feelings never spoken aloud — hidden in verses, carried in silence."
---

In shadows cast by moonlit dreams,
A heart's whispers, so it seems.
Silent echoes of a hidden flame,
In every verse, I breathe your name.

Beneath the stars, a tale untold,
Feelings wrapped in secrets, bold.
Yet, in the quiet of the night,
Hints of affection take their flight.

A gentle breeze, a soft caress,
Unspoken words, a sweet finesse.
Within the lines, a subtle trace,
Hues of love in a hidden embrace.
