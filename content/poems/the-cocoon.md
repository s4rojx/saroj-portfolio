---
title: "The Cocoon"
date: "2024-06-12"
tags: ["growth", "change", "self"]
description: "A poem about shedding the old self and emerging into something new — told through the quiet transformation of a cocoon."
---

Like a caterpillar, I spun myself in
A cocoon of silk and solitude,
A sanctuary from the shattered world
That raged outside my chrysalis.

As the rains fell and ceased to pour,
I shed my old self like a discarded skin,
Feeling out of place in a world
That no longer fit me as it had before.

Yet within me burned a flickering flame,
A fiery desire for change and growth,
A raging inferno that consumed me whole,
Burning away my fears and doubts.

Until at last the rains ceased to fall,
The storms relented, and a new dawn broke,
Revealing a world both strange and wondrous,
Full of beauty and possibility.

And from my cocoon emerged
A being more beautiful and brilliant than before,
A glorious butterfly, free and unfettered.
